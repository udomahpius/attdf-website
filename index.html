About ATTDF

Empowering Africa Through Technology and Innovation

Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is an organization registered in accordance with the laws of the federal republic of Nigeria with the objective to promote Technology Transfer(Skill Transfer), Innovation, Investment, Industrialization, Health, Agricultural, Environmental, Infrastructural, Education and Human Capital Development for massive job creation. ENVIRONMENTAL PROJECTS

ATTDF is currently working on Green Productivity Project to contribute in fighting climate change.

Green Productivity for Climate Action
Driving Sustainable Climate Solutions

Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is currently spearheading a Green Productivity Project aimed at tackling climate change through practical, sustainable solutions. This project combines environmental sustainability with economic productivity—ensuring that communities can thrive while conserving their natural ecosystems.

Promoting Eco-Innovation and Clean Technologies
Empowering Change Through Green Innovation

The Foundation actively promotes the adoption of clean, energy-efficient technologies and innovative green practices in industries and communities. By engaging stakeholders across sectors, ATTDF drives the integration of environmental considerations into core development processes, encouraging responsible resource use and waste reduction.

Community-Based Environmental Engagement
Grassroots Action for a Greener Future

ATTDF works closely with local communities to raise awareness about environmental stewardship and climate resilience. Programs include tree planting, environmental sanitation campaigns, plastic recycling awareness, and the promotion of renewable energy sources such as solar and biogas in rural and semi-urban areas.

Policy Support and Capacity Building
Building Capacity for Environmental Impact

In addition to on-the-ground projects, ATTDF contributes to policy advocacy and builds capacity among environmental officers, institutions, and youth organizations. Training and workshops are organized to align local practices with global environmental standards and to prepare a new generation of climate-conscious leaders.

ONGOING PROGRAMS

Ongoing Programs for Career Growth and Employability Enhancement

Empowering Skilled Careers Through Specialized Training & Talent Promotion From Industry-Focused Programs and Oil & Gas Business Insights to Showcasing Skilled Talent and Enhancing Employability Opportunities

Oil/Gas, Maritime and Allied Industries Career Development Programs

ATTDF in collaboration with Lagos Aviation and Maritime Business Academy, runs specialized career programs focused on fields and skills relevant in the Oil and Gas, Maritime, Aviation, and other allied industries, including international organizations. These programs are structured to provide participants with practical, high-level industry knowledge that enhances competence and boosts employability. The training is also designed to support career advancement for professionals already working in various organizations. Areas of training include engineering, management, logistics, safety, health, security, renewable energy, and other fields tailored to meet the needs of individuals or organizations.

Oil and Gas Economic Rap Session

This ongoing program is a knowledge-sharing and empowerment session that helps participants discover how to position themselves as key players in the Oil and Gas value chain. It offers direction on exploring business opportunities in the sector and provides insights that can help individuals gain a strong foothold in the industry. The session is geared toward practical exposure and guidance to build economic relevance in a thriving sector

Skilled Manpower Data Collation Initiative

ATTDF is preparing to launch a manpower data collation platform that will feature skilled and competent individuals for internship and employment visibility. The initiative is aimed at promoting trained individuals who possess both industry-relevant certifications and personal development traits such as emotional intelligence, resilience, and interpersonal skills. Those who meet the eligibility criteria can have their information featured and marketed to interested organizations and employers

Employability Promotion and Partner Collaboration

While ATTDF does not function as a recruitment or outsourcing agency, it actively supports employability by giving visibility to skilled individuals and providing relevant data to interested companies and institutions. This value-driven initiative fosters job connections and promotes human capital development. Qualified individuals are expected to reach us via our email with relevant information to participate in this employability promotion drive.

EDUCATION

ATTDF is promoting STEM education to achieve science and technical driven education that will give birth to skilled and knowledgeable manpower. The mantra to drive the concept is “Education beyond Passing Exams, getting Certificates, but for productivity and creativity enhancement for job creation”. Sub mantra “from school straight to the market place”. This inventiveness has received endorsement and partnerships by Government at federal and state levels

STEM-Driven Education for Innovation

ATTDF is championing science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education to nurture a new generation of innovative thinkers. The Foundation’s goal is to produce technically skilled manpower equipped to solve real-world problems and meet industry demands. This initiative promotes a hands-on learning approach that transforms learners into creators and solution providers

Education Beyond Certificates

With the guiding mantra, “Education beyond Passing Exams, getting Certificates, but for productivity and creativity enhancement for job creation,” ATTDF is shifting the focus from traditional classroom success to real-world applicability. The initiative emphasizes skills that promote employability, entrepreneurship, and the ability to contribute meaningfully to the economy

School to Marketplace Transition

Subtitled “From school straight to the marketplace,” ATTDF’s education model bridges the gap between academic learning and market demands. It ensures that students gain practical, in-demand skills while still in school, enabling them to transition smoothly into jobs, businesses, or industries without the common struggle of employability mismatch

Government-Endorsed Educational Reform

The innovative education model promoted by ATTDF has earned endorsements and collaboration from both federal and state governments. These partnerships help in scaling the initiative across institutions and communities, ensuring that educational reforms reach more students, especially in underrepresented areas, for broader national impact

General Information about ATTDF

What is ATTDF?

The Africa Technology Transfer and Development Foundation (ATTDF) is a Nigerian-registered organization focused on promoting technology and skill transfer, innovation, investment, and human capital development for economic growth and job creation.

When was ATTDF established?

ATTDF was founded in 2014 and has since been implementing high-knowledge-based programs across multiple sectors.
